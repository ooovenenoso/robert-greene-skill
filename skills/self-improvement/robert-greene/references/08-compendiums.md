## Compendium 3: Map of laws (complements and tensions)

### Pairs that complement each other

Laws that together are stronger than apart:

- **3 + 4** (Conceal intentions + Say less): mystery is built with absence of information
- **1 + 14** (Don't outshine + Imitate boss): shining with his light + being his reflection
- **7 + 8** (Take credit through others + Carrot): yield the spotlight but reward who yields
- **12 + 13** (Selective honesty + Self-interest): raw truth + clear benefit = unbeatable combo
- **15 + 23** (Crush totally + Concentrate): learn from the best + focus = fast mastery
- **22 + 47** (Surrender + Don't go past target): know when to stop, know when to release
- **27 + 37** (Cult + Spectacle): create meaning + dramatize it = religion
- **34 + 39** (King + Stir emotions): posture of command + emotional speech = total leadership
- **40 + 42** (Charge + Diversify income): he who charges his value is never a slave
- **41 + 48** (Diversify + Flow): multiple sources + constant adaptation

### Pairs in tension

Laws that seem to contradict. Mastery is choosing which to apply in each moment:

| Pair | Tension | When to use each |
|------|---------|------------------|
| **1 vs 6** (don't outshine vs court attention) | Humility vs visibility | 1 with your direct boss; 6 with the world |
| **3 vs 5** (conceal vs reputation) | Mystery vs public clarity | 3 in plans; 5 in built image |
| **10 vs 11** (avoid unhappy vs dependency) | Avoid vs connect | 10 with toxic; 11 with valuable network |
| **20 vs 27** (no commitments vs create cult) | Freedom vs belonging | 20 in politics; 27 with your chosen tribe |
| **22 vs 47** (surrender vs don't stop) | Yield vs continue | 22 when battle is lost; 47 when at victory |
| **25 vs 38** (renew vs conventional) | Change vs stability | 25 in life strategy; 38 in daily execution |
| **30 vs 46** (show effort vs don't be perfect) | Vulnerability vs image | 30 shows the path; 46 shows minor flaws |
| **36 vs 45** (disdain unattainable vs ask big) | Indifference vs ambition | 36 with genuinely impossible; 45 with negotiable |

### Fundamental triads

Three laws together cover a complete scenario:

- **ENTRY triad** (how to enter a new space): Law 6, Law 14, Law 34
- **NEGOTIATION triad** (how to get what you want): Law 9, Law 13, Law 45
- **CRISIS triad** (how to survive conflict): Law 19, Law 22, Law 26
- **BRAND-BUILDING triad** (how to be known): Law 5, Law 27, Law 37
- **LEADERSHIP triad** (how to lead): Law 7, Law 8, Law 39

### Deep antinomies

Pairs where only **context** and **maturity** tell you which to apply:

- **Conceal vs Show**: when to conceal (3, 21, 24, 36) vs when to show (5, 6, 26, 30, 46). Rule: conceal strategy, show character.
- **Wait vs Act**: when to wait (15, 16, 22, 35) vs when to act (9, 28, 45). Rule: wait for the right moment, execute with everything.
- **Be vs Not be**: when to be yourself (24, 38, 46) vs when to be chameleon (14, 21, 38). Rule: in private be authentic; in public, strategic.
- **Give vs Receive**: when to give (7, 12, 27, 40) vs when to receive (8, 13, 42). Rule: give first in new relationships; receive in mature alliances.
